About Me

Hey, I'm Nicholas Pretli. When it comes to the "Nicholas" or "Nick" debate, honestly you do you! Most people I know call me Nick, though I get Nicholas a lot, too - especially for more formal matters or in writing.

Recently, and like many others this year, I've embarked on some additional cooking and baking at home which led to a personal binder full of recipes for me to carry through life. I try to keep a good book on hand, whether fiction or non-fiction (though I'm conviced I prefer non-fiction; not sure why).
