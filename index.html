About Our Client

Founded in 1992, FeedOntario is a multi-service agency that oversees food banks across the province. Their programs engage in food distribution & funding, research, and public education efforts. Each year, Feed Ontario publishes a hunger report which provides an overview of the current state hunger needs across Ontario. In their 2019 report, they noted an increasing trend in the number of clients visiting food banks.
